# Helpful Resources

This page links to official Shopify resources and Staylime support to help you get the most out of the Volume theme.

### Shopify Documentation

* [Theme Editor Guide](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/online-store/themes/theme-structure/theme-editor) — how to use the Shopify Theme Editor
* [Languages & Translations](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/markets/languages) — managing store languages
* [Translate & Adapt App](https://apps.shopify.com/translate-and-adapt) — Shopify's official translation app
* [Shopify Markets](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/markets) — sell in multiple regions and currencies
* [Search & Discovery App](https://apps.shopify.com/search-and-discovery) — configure search, filters, and product recommendations
* [Metafields & Metaobjects](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/custom-data) — add custom data to products, collections, and pages
* [Shopify Email](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/promoting-marketing/shopify-email) — email marketing built into Shopify
* [Speed Optimization Guide](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/online-store/themes/speed-optimization) — tips for improving your store's loading speed
* [Shopify YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@shopify) — tutorials, demos, and tips from Shopify
* [Shopify Community Forums](https://community.shopify.com/) — get help from other merchants and developers

### Staylime Support
