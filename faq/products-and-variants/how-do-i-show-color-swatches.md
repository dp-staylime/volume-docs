# How do I show color swatches?

1. Go to **Theme Editor → Theme settings → Variant selector**
2. Scroll to **Color pills**
3. In the **Variant label** field, enter the exact name of your color option as it appears in your product (e.g. `Color`, `Colour`, `Finish`)
4. Choose a **Style** — **Swatch** for color circles, **Image** for product thumbnails
