# Product Card

Product Card settings control how individual products appear in collection grids, search results, and featured product sections across your store.

### Card Layout

Choose the overall structure of the product card:

* **Portrait** — tall card with image on top, details below (most common)
* **Landscape** — wide card with image on the left, details on the right
* **Overlay** — product details appear over the image on hover

### Image Settings

| Setting               | Description                                                              |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Image ratio           | Aspect ratio — Square (1:1), Portrait (3:4), Landscape (4:3), or Natural |
| Image fit             | Cover (crop to fill) or Contain (show full image)                        |
| Second image on hover | Show alternate product image when customer hovers                        |

📸 SCREENSHOT: Product card with hover image effect

### Product Info Display

Control which elements appear on the card:

* **Vendor** — show/hide brand name above product title
* **Title** — always shown
* **Price** — always shown; configure compare-at price display
* **Rating** — show star rating if reviews app is installed
* **Color swatches** — show available color variants as inline swatches
* **Quick add button** — add to cart without opening the product page

📸 SCREENSHOT: Product card with all info elements visible

### Text Alignment

Set text alignment for card details: Left, Center, or Right.

### Card Hover Effect

Choose what happens when a customer hovers over a card:

* **None** — no effect
* **Lift** — card rises with a shadow
* **Zoom** — product image scales up slightly
