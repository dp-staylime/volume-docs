Upload a custom favicon shown in browser tabs and bookmarks.

**Image** — upload a PNG file. Automatically scaled to 32 × 32 px.

{% hint style="success" icon="lightbulb" %}
**Tip:** Use a PNG with a transparent background for best results across light and dark browser tabs.
{% endhint %}
