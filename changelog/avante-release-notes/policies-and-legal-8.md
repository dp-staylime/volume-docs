# Avante 6.0.0

#### **Released November, 2024** <a href="#released-april-9-2026" id="released-april-9-2026"></a>

### Added

* **Free shipping progress bar:** Show customers how close they are to free shipping in the cart and cart drawer. Enable in Theme settings → Cart. See Cart drawer guide.
* **Countdown timers in banners:** Add a Countdown timer block to Announcement Bar, Slideshow, Image Banner, and Media with text sections.
* **Highlight menu items:** Mark specific navigation items (e.g. "Sale") with a visual highlight. Configure in the Header, Main sidebar, or Mega menu block settings.
