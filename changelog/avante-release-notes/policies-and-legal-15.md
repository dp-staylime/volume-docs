# Avante 1.2.0

#### **Released November, 2023** <a href="#released-april-9-2026" id="released-april-9-2026"></a>

### Added

* **Image comparison section:** Let customers drag to compare two images side by side — useful for before/after or product detail views.
* **Image with hotspots section:** Create interactive images with clickable pins that show popups — useful for product feature highlights or room setups.
