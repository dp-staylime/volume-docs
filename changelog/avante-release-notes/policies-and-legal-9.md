# Avante 5.2.0

#### **Released September, 2024** <a href="#released-april-9-2026" id="released-april-9-2026"></a>

### Added

* **Sticky Add to Cart:** A persistent buy button that stays visible while customers scroll the product page. Enable in the Product overview section settings.
* **Accelerated checkout buttons:** Shop Pay, Apple Pay, and Google Pay buttons now appear directly on the product page.
* **Language switcher in mobile header:** Add a language selector block to the mobile header.
